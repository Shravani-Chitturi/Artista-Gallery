SHRAVANI CHITTURI


Welcome to the vibrant world of Shravani, where imagination meets canvas to create awe-inspiring works of art. Born and raised in Ballari, Shravani discovered their passion for art at a young age, influenced by the rich cultural tapestry of their surroundings. Drawing inspiration from Banksy, their artistic journey has evolved into a captivating exploration of Street and Urban Art.
